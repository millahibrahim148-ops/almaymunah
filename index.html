The Correct Methodology in Seeking Knowledge

Seeking knowledge is from the greatest acts of worship, and it is not attained except with sincerity and patience.

The Salaf used to travel long distances, endure hunger, and abandon comfort for the sake of preserving the Sunnah.

Knowledge is not merely memorization, but understanding, acting upon it, and calling others with wisdom.
