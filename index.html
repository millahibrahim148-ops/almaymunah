Seeking Knowledge with Sincerity

| Uploaded by Al Maymunah

Knowledge is among the greatest blessings bestowed upon mankind. It guides, protects, and elevates hearts. The seeker of knowledge should purify his intention, aiming solely for the pleasure of Allah, not worldly gain.

Scholars have emphasized sincerity in seeking knowledge, reminding us that knowledge without intention is like a tree without fruit.
