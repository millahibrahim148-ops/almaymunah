The Correct Methodology in Seeking Knowledge
12 Rabīʿ al-Ākhir 1446H

Seeking knowledge is among the greatest acts of worship, yet it is only accepted when it is built upon sincerity and correct methodology. The Salaf emphasized beginning with the foundations before the branches, and acting upon knowledge before calling others to it.
