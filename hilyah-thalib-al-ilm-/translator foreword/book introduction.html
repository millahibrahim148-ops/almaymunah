Description of the Book

The Book: Hilyah Ṭālib al-ʿIlm - Etiquettes of a Student on Seeking Knowledge

Author: Bakr bin Abdullah Abu Zaid (d. 1429 AH)

Translated by: Abu ’Abdillah Murad ibn Hilmi Ash-Shuweik

Publisher: Dar al-Asima, Riyadh

Number of pages: 97

Contents

Translator's Foreword
Author's Foreword
Chapter 1: The Etiquette of the Seeker's Inner Self
1. Knowledge is worship 2. Be adherent to the path of the Salaf as-Saalih 3. Commit to fearing Allaah, the Most High
Chapter 2: The Etiquette with One’s Teacher
1. Honour and respect your teacher 2. Do not precede him in speech 3. Lower your voice in his presence
