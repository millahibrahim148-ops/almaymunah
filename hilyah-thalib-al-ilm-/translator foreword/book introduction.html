Welcome to Hilyah Ṭālib al-ʿIlm

This is the English translation of the renowned book Hilyah Ṭālib al-ʿIlm, a classical work dedicated to outlining the etiquettes of seeking knowledge in Islaam.

The book contains timeless guidance for students of knowledge, reminding them of sincerity, patience, humility, and dedication on their path.

You may begin reading from the Translator’s Foreword, which introduces the context, purpose, and importance of this work.
