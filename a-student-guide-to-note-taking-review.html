A Student’s Guide to Note-Taking & Review

Proper note-taking is an essential skill for a student of knowledge. It allows one to capture, organize, and review knowledge efficiently.

1. The Purpose of Notes

Notes are not just for recording words but for capturing understanding. They serve as a tool to remember, reflect, and apply.

2. Methods of Note-Taking

  • Linear Notes: Writing point by point in sequence.
  • Mind Maps: Visual diagrams connecting topics.
  • Margin Notes: Adding personal comments or references.

3. Reviewing Notes

Knowledge becomes firm with review. Set aside dedicated times to revisit your notes, summarize them, and test yourself.

4. Connecting with Scholars

Notes should complement learning from teachers and authentic sources. Always verify, refine, and annotate your notes with reliable references.

May Allah grant us beneficial knowledge and allow us to act upon it.
