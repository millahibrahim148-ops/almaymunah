Ta’iyyah of Abu Ishaq al-Ilbirī
Beginner Advice Poetry
Written by the Andalusian scholar Abu Ishāq al-Ilbīrī, this Tā’iyyah addresses Abu Bakr with beneficial advice, reminders and admonitions. It seeks to soften the reader’s heart, awaken from heedlessness, and inspire striving for the Hereafter.
